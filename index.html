Marcello Lucchini

Welcome to the Zaudke FINE ART OF ITALY gallery where you will find a great collection of Italian paintings by artist, Marcello Lucchini. Frequent visits are made to Italy in order to explore the many well-known areas as well as those off the beaten path. Marcello expresses his love of the Italian scenery, culture, and history through hhis paintings. This gallery offers a sampling of the many regions of Italy that he has traveled.
